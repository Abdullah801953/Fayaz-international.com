Study MBBS in Armenia

The medical profession is considered one of the noblest in the world. Medical Sciences is a prestigious field, so an MBBS degree requires extensive and challenging education. Most students hope to pass the degree entrance exam, but very few are selected.

Armenia has long been one of the most popular destinations for studying MBBS. Due to its advanced education system and top-tier infrastructure, Armenia has a lot to offer for scientific research.

At present, Armenia has 26 state universities (four of which are international) and 33 private (licensed) institutions of higher education. Universities, institutes, academies, and conservatories are some of the types of institutions that provide higher education. Physicists, mathematicians, chemists, microbiologists, engineers, medical professionals, and IT specialists can all study in Armenia.

Practical training is given paramount importance in Armenian education, and a majority of universities now prioritize practical training over classroom lessons. Armenia has set the standard for the educational system of most countries as a developed nation. Armenia does not require any other entrance examination besides NEET for MBBS students.

About MBBS In Armenia

Armenia is located deep inside the northern part of the subtropical zone, surrounded by lofty mountains, so its climate is dry and continental. There is nevertheless considerable regional climate variation.

About 95 percent of the population is Armenian; they speak Armenian, an Indo-European language. Kurds, Russians, Assyrians, and other minorities make up the remainder of the population. The city's diverse population is what attracts most people. Fresh graduates are finding employment in the country as it is in a developing state and has many employment opportunities Buses and trains connect the country. Slowly, the country is moving towards a fast-paced and urbanized lifestyle. However, its ancient roots remain intact and will last for a very long time

MBBS In Armenia For Indian Students

Armenia is a very good option for Indian medical students wishing to pursue their MBBS. Its infrastructure is very advanced and its teaching is of European standards, which are accepted worldwide. After passing their MCI and FMGE screening tests, MBBS candidates can work in India. Additionally, Armenian universities award MBBS degrees that are accepted worldwide and many prestigious doctors have graduated and are working in some of the world's best hospitals.

Culture in Armenia is very easily adaptable, and students are provided with excessive comfort. Students from India will benefit from the fact that there is already a large number of Indian students in Armenian universities, so they won't feel out of place There are no language barriers in Armenian universities, so studying there is a plus. A good advantage for students is the availability of Indian food virtually everywhere. There is also a good selection of vegetarian food available in the country for those who prefer it. The opportunity to witness a whole new culture in Armenia will be a great opportunity for students to explore something outside of India. In addition to being beautiful, the country is also very advanced.

Cost of Study For MBBS In Armenia 2023-24

MBBS in Armenia is preferred by many students because of its low tuition fees. However, the quality of education is comparable to that of top universities around the world. The cost of studying and tuition fees varies from university to university. Students pay not only for education but also for world-class amenities provided by the university. On average, university tuition ranges from Rs. 1.75 lakhs to Rs. 3 lakhs each year. Despite the fact that this might be high for some, this is an investment for a better future.

Food and Accommodation Cost

Armenia offers both hostels and rented apartments for international students. In addition, students can stay in a system called a "host family" where they are invited by locals to stay with them, thus getting an authentic taste of Armenian culture. Hostel fees for American universities vary from university to university. The cost of renting an apartment can range anywhere between Rs. 10,000 and Rs. 45,000 depending on where you live. Student hostels provide food for their students, but apartment residents need to purchase their own food. There are grocery stores in every corner of every city, as well as many places that do monthly food delivery packages for students especially.

Living Cost

A person's lifestyle will determine how much they spend on living expenses. Depending on the accommodation, food, utilities, and other necessities, international students in Armenia may spend between Rs. 42,224 and 54,508. Furthermore, students need to purchase university books and study materials, which might cost between Rs. 30,000 and Rs. 40,000.

Travelling cost

Universities in Armenia have vastly different travel costs depending on the state one chooses to study in. There are underground metro systems in big cities like Yerevan, but in smaller towns, taxis and buses are more common. There is easy access to the campus from those who stay on the campus. People who commute regularly can benefit from bus and train passes, which are available to everyone. In addition, students can rent a car if they have a valid license.

Admission Procedure For Armenia MBBS College

Step 1: Before filling application forms, candidates have to apply for the NEET entrance exam.

Step 2: Once the results of these tests are available. You will need to fill out an online application provided by the university.

Step 3: Print out your form. Send a scanned copy of the completed form to the University, along with the documents listed below.

Step 4: Scan the documents and send them by email with the subject line stating your intent, i.e. 'Application for Admission'.

Step 5: Documents can only be submitted before the application deadline.

Step 6: After the University has verified the student's documents, the student will receive an acceptance letter or rejection letter, depending on what the University decides.

MBBS In Armenia Eligibility
  • The students must have completed their SSC and HSC, also known as 10+2.
  • Biology, physics, and chemistry should be studied in high school.
  • Applicants must complete their 17th year of age by the 31st of December in the year of admission.
  • Applicants must score at least 50% in their HSC boards from a recognized board of examination.
  • Candidates must pass the NEET examination.
Scholarship Offered For MBBS In Armenia

Scholarships are awarded by Armenian universities as a form of financial aid to deserving students. Armenian colleges offer medical students many opportunities for global scholarships. Therefore, Indian students stand a good chance of receiving grants or scholarships.

Scholarships available to Armenian students include the SIT Zero Fees English Scholarship, Education Future International Scholarship 23, And Sir Ratanji Dalal Research Scholarship.

Frequently Asked Question
  • What is the duration of MBBS in Armenia?
    MBBS duration in the Armenia is of about 5.5 years which includes 1 year of compulsory internship
  • Is it possible to pursue MBBS in Armenia without giving the NEET?
    No, NEET is mandatory.
  • Is there on campus accommodation available?
    Yes, almost all universities provide campus accommodation to their students. If a student isn’t provided with one or they are having issues with it, there are many rental apartments available for them.
  • Are there any job opportunities after completing MBBS in Armenia?
    Yes, there are job opportunities. Candidates can work in the many well-known hospitals in the Armenia
  • Is staying in Armenia too expensive?
    When expenses are calculated properly, there is no great cost involved. The lifestyle of an individual is also important. Universities do, however, offer paid internships and even financial aid in some cases.
  • Do I need to take any other entrance exams besides NEET?
    No.
  • Is the Visa Process complicated?
    No, it isn’t. Once University provides the invitation letter, students can apply for the visa at the embassy
  • Will I be able to work anywhere else other than Armenia after completion of the program?
    Yes, Universities in Armenia are internationally recognized and students of the University have worked in several countries around the globe.
