MBBS in South Korea for Indian Students

South Korea is emerging as a new popular destination to study for Indian students. MBBS in South Korea for Indian students is an ideal option for medical aspirants as it would be cost-friendly. Students who wish to become proficient doctors with excellent skills can choose to pursue a medical degree in South Korea. MBBS in South Korea is a 6-year-long medical degree. In which undergraduate education is provided in the first 2 years. The following years include pre-clinical studies in the 3rd and 4th years and the last years include practical training. Through this article, let’s learn more about how one can pursue MBBS in South Korea. 

Why Study MBBS in South Korea?

The following are the reasons to pursue MBBS in South Korea for Indian students:

  • South Korea has one of the world’s highest-educated labour force 
  • Studying MBBS in South Korea for Indian students would be highly affordable compared to the other countries
  • Universities in South Korea aim to provide top-quality education to students
  • Being one of the most technologically advanced nations, education in South Korea will enable students to practice advanced technology and learn new medical techniques 
  • Students will get the opportunity to learn from the best industry professionals and will have a deeper understanding of medical sciences
  • Many universities in South Korea teach medical courses in the English language as well to provide a better understanding to international students
  • South Korea provides students with a safe environment to study 
Benefits of Studying MBBS in South Korea

The following are the benefits of studying MBBS in South Korea for Indian students:

  • Many universities provide affordable education
  • Studying in South Korea will enable students to explore global opportunities
  • The country provides a safe and secure environment for students
  • Most South Korean universities provide English medium courses 
  • Students will get the opportunity to get hands-on experience and practical knowledge
  • Students may not be required to take an entrance test to get admission 
  • Medical professionals in South Korea are paid generously

Eligibility Criteria 

The following are the eligibility criteria to pursue MBBS in South Korea for Indian students:

  • Indian Pre- University: The candidate must have completed 10+2 with 70% aggregate and objects including Physics or Mathematics, Chemistry and  Biology)
  • STPM/ A -Levels: The candidate must have qualified with grades BBB, ABC or AAC in subjects Biology, Chemistry, Physics or Mathematics 
  • The candidate must provide their English Language Proficiency test score: IELTS – 6.0 or TOEFL – 550
Documents Required

The following are the documents required to pursue MBBS in South Korea:

  • A valid passport 
  • Academic Marksheet of 10th and 12th
  • Birth Certificate 
  • Passport size photographs
  • Offer letter from a medical university in South Korea
  • Documents authorised by the Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi 
  • Documents with legalization for the South Korea Embassy
  • Application fees and Visa
  • Bank receipt for the first year tuition fee of the university 
Application Process

The following are the steps to apply for MBBS in South  Korea for Indian students:

  • Visit the selected university website to check the specific university eligibility criteria 
  • Fill out the application form and provide the details accurately
  • When received an offer letter, save it for future use
  • Pay the expense fee of the university 
  • Apply for the visa after the payment has been made to the university 
  • After the visa application has been made, prepare for accommodation and flights booking 
Cost of Studying 

Studying MBBS in South Korea for Indian students is highly affordable compared to other cities. The fee to study MBBS in South Korea is $5128 per semester or INR 4.18 Lakh.
