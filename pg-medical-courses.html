PG Medical Courses

Duration : 3 Years

Tuition Fee : Approx 3-5 Lakh/Year

Eligibility

1. The candidate must have passed MBBS examination from any Medical College recognized by Medical Council of India.

2. The candidates should posses a permanent registration with any State Medical Council in India or Medical Council of India. If he does not have the

same he will be required to obtain such registration within one month after admission to the P. G. course, failing which, his /her admission to the P. G. course will be cancelled.

3. The candidate must have completed the compulsory rotating internship on or before 30th Apr & have to produce completion certificate before joining the course.
